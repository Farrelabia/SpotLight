# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Young adults (18-30) in Jabodetabek who want to discover popular places. They browse casually on their phones, often while commuting or hanging out with friends. They care about aesthetics and want curated recommendations, not exhaustive listings.

## Product Purpose

SpotLight helps users find trendy cafes, museums, food spots, and parks in the Jabodetabek area. Success means a user opens the app, quickly finds an appealing place, and visits it. The experience should feel like a trusted friend recommending their favorite spots.

## Brand Personality

Refined, polished, trustworthy. Elegant without being pretentious. Clean with quiet confidence. Think Airbnb: curated, premium, warm but sophisticated.

## Anti-references

- Crowded and noisy interfaces with too many colors competing for attention
- Corporate or boring layouts that feel stiff and institutional
- Generic Android Studio template aesthetics that scream "tutorial project"

## Design Principles

1. **Curated over comprehensive.** Show fewer things, better presented. Quality of presentation signals quality of recommendation.
2. **Let the places shine.** Photos and content are the hero. The UI steps back and frames them beautifully.
3. **Quiet confidence.** No shouting, no neon, no excessive decoration. Polish comes from restraint and precision, not embellishment.
4. **Touch-friendly warmth.** Despite being refined, it should feel approachable and human, not cold or clinical.
5. **Consistent vocabulary.** Same component language across every screen. If a card looks one way here, it looks that way everywhere.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG AA minimum
- Touch targets meet 48dp minimum
- Proper content descriptions for images
- Sufficient color contrast for all text
